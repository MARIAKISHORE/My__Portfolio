üéØ Customize Your Portfolio

Update Your Information

Open index.html with a text editor and find:

  • Your Name: Replace "Maria Kishore" in the hero section
  • Your Title: Update the subtitle with your professional title
  • About Text: Add your personal introduction
  • Skills: List your technical skills
  • Experience: Add your work history
  • Projects: Showcase your best projects
  • Contact Info: Add your email, phone, and social links

Change Colors

Open styles.css and edit these variables at the top:

:root { --primary-color: #6366f1; /* Change this */ --secondary-color: #8b5cf6; /* Change this */ --accent-color: #ec4899; /* Change this */ }

Add Your Profile Picture

In the About section of index.html, replace:

<img src="https://via.placeholder.com/300x300">

With your own image URL or file path
